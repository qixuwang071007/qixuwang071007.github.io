Hello! I am a researcher and academic specializing in robotics and control systems. Currently, I work as a Research Assistant III at the SDSU Research Foundation in San Diego.

Research Interests

My research actively focuses on the safety and stability analysis of uncrewed systems, including unicycle models and underwater robotics such as the TurtleBot3 and the BlueROV. I am particularly interested in:

  • Autonomous Systems & Motion Planning
  • Extremum Seeking Control (ESC)
  • Logarithmic Barrier Functions
  • Singular Perturbations for Robot Source Seeking

Technical Expertise

In my engineering practice and simulation validation, I frequently utilize a modern robotics toolchain, including ROS 2 Humble and Gazebo.
