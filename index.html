Research Interests

My research actively focuses on the safety and stability analysis of uncrewed systems, including unicycle models and underwater robotics. I am particularly interested in:

  • Autonomous Systems & Motion Planning
  • Extremum Seeking Control (ESC)
  • Robot Source Seeking

Technical Expertise

In my engineering practice and simulation validation, I frequently utilize a modern robotics toolchain, including ROS 2 Humble and Gazebo. In our experiments, we actively deploy and test algorithms using the TurtleBot3 and BlueROV2 platforms.
